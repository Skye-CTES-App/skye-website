The Problem
Every weather app is built to alarm you.

40 million Americans have anxiety disorders. Millions more experience fear during severe weather. But every weather app is built the same way: scary red alerts, complex radar, and zero emotional support. Nobody answers the question anxious people are actually asking.

🚨
SEVERE WARNING
Alarm-driven alerts that spike your cortisol instead of helping you understand the situation.
📡
Raw Radar Data
Complex maps designed for meteorologists, not for someone panicking at 2am.
😰
No Reassurance
No app tells you "your home can handle this" or "this passes in 35 minutes."
🧘
No Storm Tools
Wellness apps have breathing exercises but zero connection to live weather data.
The Solution
Skye translates weather data into emotional clarity.

Real-time weather intelligence meets psychology-informed communication. Not a weather app. Not a meditation app. Something entirely new.

🛡
Am I Safe? Button
One tap. Real-time location data. A clear, honest answer based on current conditions — not a generic alert.
💬
Live Storm Companion
Stays with you through the entire storm. Calm updates, progress countdown, and a voice that says "you're doing well" when you need it most.
⚡
Calm Forecast Mode
Plain-language risk levels. "Loud thunder expected. Your home is fine. This passes in 35 minutes." Not SEVERE THUNDERSTORM WARNING in red.
🌬
Anxiety Toolkit
Breathing timer, 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, storm facts, and a post-storm journal. Evidence-based tools that actually work during panic.
⚙️
Fear Profile
Tell Skye what scares you — tornadoes, lightning, thunder, being alone. Every alert adapts to YOUR triggers, not generic warnings.
